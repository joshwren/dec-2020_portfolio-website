About Me....

Graphic Designer andWeb Developer

Hi, my name is Joshua Wren and I am a highly motivated and courageous Graphic Designer, Signage/Wayfinding Designer and Web Developer, living in Canberra ACT. I am extremely interested in all parts of design, but particularly enjoy publication design, infographic design and web design/development in general...

I have a Bachelor Degree in Graphic Design & Multi-Media acquired with my studies through the University of Canberra (UC). Along with that I posses an Advanced Diploma in Graphic Design & Digital Media studied through Canberra Institute of Technology (CIT).
I am currently studying for a Bachelor Degree in Web Design and Production through the University of Canberra (UC) in order to develop my skills and experience within digital design and development.
I have been in the design industry for over ten plus years, three and a half years within signage design/production and eight plus years within print and digital design.
